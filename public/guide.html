Introduction

Welcome to the Notes App! This guide will walk you through the key features and how to use them effectively.

Creating Notes

  1. Click on the "Add Note" button in the app.
  2. Enter your note text in the provided textarea.
  3. Add tags to categorize your note (optional).
  4. Click the "Save" button to create the note.

Searching Notes

  1. Use the search input field to enter keywords or phrases.
  2. The app will instantly display notes that match your search query.
  3. You can also filter notes by selecting specific tags from the tag dropdown.

Editing Notes

  1. Click on the note you want to edit.
  2. Make the necessary changes to the note text or tags.
  3. Click the "Save" button to update the note.

Deleting Notes

  1. Click on the note you want to delete.
  2. Click the "Delete" button to remove the note.
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
